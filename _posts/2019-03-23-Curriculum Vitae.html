--- layout: post title: "Curriculum Vitae" date: 2024-09-06 categories: CV --- ### About Me Hi, thanks for visiting my site. I conducted research at the Functional Analysis Lab of [Sejong University](http://sejong.ac.kr/). I am currently a graduate student in [Causality Lab](https://causality.snu.ac.kr/) at [Seoul National University](https://www.snu.ac.kr/). ### Research My research focuses on Causal Decision Making and Causal Effect Identification. I study how to graphically model a wide range of real-world problems in a causal manner and make optimal decisions. In particular, my research explores methods for making robust decisions, even when modeling with incomplete information. Additionally, I am investigating decision making processes in sequential settings.
